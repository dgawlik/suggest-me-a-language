Expressiveness

  • does it have powerful abstractions?
Conciseness
  • 1 to 10
  • how long is code comparable to other languages?

Ecosystem stength
  • 1 to 10
  • is the audience growing?

Community support
  • 1 to 10
  • how many posts on StackOverflow?

  • does community participate in language development?
Maturity
  • 1 to 10
